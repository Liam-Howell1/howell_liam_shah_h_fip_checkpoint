Our patches come in five Amstel alcohol varieties. Whether you’re looking for a lager patch, a pilsener patch or a light patch we have everything for your alcohol needs. Our alcohol is not concentrated with any other products so you can be assured you are getting the purest of alcoholic relief from these patches.

The patches also come in two varieties, face patches for those who don’t mind having the patches applied to their face and body patches for those who wish to keep it a little more hidden from the rest of the people you are around.

We store our patches in a bottle that resembles a beer bottle. This gives our customers the satisfaction of being close to their bottle but not having the actual drink and instead patches that they can apply with the alcohol they want.

Our design team has made our patches biodegradable to help out the environment. We have designed the patches so that after they have been used they will decompose and remain eco friendly leaving no harmful waste behind. They are compost friendly and can be put in your gardens in the nicer weather to decompose there and help your plants.

While these patches aren’t the same as having an open alcohol container in public you are still required to follow the public alcohol laws including legal drinking age. You’re blood alcohol levels will still show after using our product and can still give you the same buzz as a beverage if used in succession. Be responsible and follow the same rules of drinking when using our patches.
